ABOUT US

In the heart of the Indian subcontinent, where the bustling Delhi-Mumbai highway carves its path through the countryside, there stood a humble dhaba that would soon become a legend in its own right. It was 1977 when Mr. Kishan Lal ji, a man with a vision and a palate for authentic Punjabi cuisine, set up his modest eatery, Haryana Hotel, at Mudasa. As the wheels of time turned, so did the fortunes of Haryana Hotel. The aroma of spices, the sizzle of tandoori, and the warmth of hospitality drew travelers from far and wide. Mr. Kishan Lal's passion for food and dedication to his craft turned his dhaba into a sanctuary for weary travelers and food enthusiasts alike. In 1984, the baton of this culinary legacy was passed on to Mr. Kishan Lal's elder son, Mr. Pankaj Dhingra. With his father's recipes and guidance, Pankaj stepped into the world of food business. His dedication and commitment to maintaining the standards set by his father turned out to be the secret ingredient that kept Haryana Hotel shining.

When We Started

After the closure of their beloved dhaba, Haryana Hotel, Mr. Pankaj and Mr. Jugesh found themselves longing to reignite their passion for food. Despite working in different industries, their love for serving delicious Punjabi cuisine never faded. In 2017, their culinary dreams took flight once again with the establishment of the iconic Namaste Punjab. Starting as a cloud kitchen, Namaste Punjab quickly gained popularity for its authentic Punjabi dishes and cuisine. The flavors of Punjab, perfected over generations, found new life in the kitchen of Namaste Punjab. The aroma of spices, the sizzle of tandoori, and the warmth of Punjabi hospitality filled the air once again. The success of Namaste Punjab skyrocketed, attracting the attention of various online food delivery platforms. Recognizing the immense potential, these platforms expressed interest in investing to open Namaste Punjab dine-in outlets. From a small cloud kitchen to a burgeoning culinary empire, Namaste Punjab's journey is a testament to the unwavering passion and dedication of Mr. Pankaj and Mr. Jugesh to bring the authentic flavors of Punjab to the world.
